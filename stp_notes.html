MBA Marketing Notes

Quick Revision with Examples

Market Segmentation

Definition: Dividing market into smaller consumer groups based on similar needs or characteristics.

  • Geographic: region, city size
  • Demographic: age, gender, income
  • Psychographic: lifestyle, personality
  • Behavioural: benefits sought, usage rate

Example (India): Coca Cola – different pack sizes for rural and urban markets.

Consumer Behaviour

Definition: Study of how individuals, groups select, use, and dispose products to satisfy needs.

  • Influenced by cultural, social, personal, psychological factors.

Example (India): Patanjali appeals to health-conscious, Ayurveda-trusting consumers.

Marketing Mix

Definition: Combination of Product, Price, Place, Promotion to market a product effectively.

  • Product: quality, design
  • Price: MRP, discounts
  • Place: distribution channels
  • Promotion: ads, sales promotion

Example (India): Amul Ice Cream – good product, affordable price, wide distribution, TV ads.
